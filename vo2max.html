What's Inside

Eight weeks. Four focused sections.

01

How VO2max Works

The physiology behind aerobic capacity, explained for athletes who want to understand what they're training, not just follow instructions. Covers the cardiovascular and muscular factors that determine your ceiling, why it matters beyond just running performance, and what realistic improvement looks like over an 8-week block.

02

The 8-Week Protocol

Two sessions per week. Week-by-week progression using interval-based training methods proven to drive VO2max adaptation. Each session is written with warm-up, work intervals, and recovery structure. Designed to fit within a hybrid training schedule without displacing your strength work or accumulating excessive fatigue.

03

Fitting It Into Your Week

How to schedule your VO2max sessions around lifting days, recovery days and your existing endurance work. The interference effect is real — the guide explains how to manage it through session sequencing, same-day ordering, and load management so your strength training and aerobic work reinforce rather than undermine each other.

04

Tracking Progress

What metrics to monitor over the 8 weeks and how to interpret them. VO2max estimates from wearables, pace-at-threshold data, heart rate response at fixed intensities, and subjective effort ratings. Know whether the protocol is working — and what to adjust if it isn't — before the 8 weeks are up.

A Few Things Worth Knowing
  • This is an 8-week protocol, not a long-term program. It's designed to run as a focused block within your broader training — after which you return to your standard structure or move into a maintenance phase.
  • It requires two interval-focused sessions per week. If your schedule can't consistently accommodate this, the protocol won't work as written.
  • This is a digital PDF. You'll receive a download link by email immediately after purchase.
  • It's designed for athletes with a base level of aerobic fitness. If you can't currently run for 30 minutes continuously, build that base first before running this protocol.
  • If you want a more complete system — one that integrates VO2max training within a full 12-week program built around both strength and endurance — that's what the Hybrid Athlete Blueprint covers.

VO2max is one of the most trainable metrics in performance. Eight weeks of the right structure makes a measurable difference.
