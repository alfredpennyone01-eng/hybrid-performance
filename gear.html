Heart Rate Monitoring

GPS Running Watch

The cornerstone of any data-driven training setup. Optical HRV tracking, VO2max estimates, training load and recovery scores. Non-negotiable for hybrid athletes who need to manage cumulative fatigue.

Shop on Amazon
Chest Strap HRM

Wrist-based optical HR is adequate for easy runs. For interval sessions and VO2max work where precision matters, a chest strap is consistently more accurate and worth having in your kit.

Shop on Amazon
HRV Monitoring App

A dedicated morning HRV measurement protocol gives you a daily readiness signal that's far more actionable than watch-based estimates. Pairs with a chest strap for sub-60-second morning readings.

Recovery Tools

Foam Roller

The basic version is fine. Long-form soft tissue work before bed or after sessions for hip flexors, IT band, calves and thoracic spine. Takes five minutes and makes the next day's session feel significantly better.

Shop on Amazon
Massage Gun

Fast, targeted tissue work for glutes, hamstrings and calves between sessions. Not a replacement for sleep and nutrition, but useful when time between sessions is tight and localised fatigue is real.

Sleep Tracker

Sleep is the highest-leverage recovery tool available. A dedicated ring or band gives you accurate sleep staging data that integrates with your HRV morning readings for a complete recovery picture.
