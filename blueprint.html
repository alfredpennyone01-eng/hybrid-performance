What's Inside

Four modules. One complete system.

01

12-Week Training Plan

A week-by-week strength and endurance schedule built around interference effect research. Each week is structured to maximise both adaptations, not compromise between them. Strength sessions are sequenced around endurance work to minimise residual fatigue. Load progresses deliberately, with planned deload weeks built in rather than forced on you by exhaustion.

02

Nutrition Guide

Practical fuelling strategies for athletes doing two things at once. Covers pre-training nutrition for dual sessions, protein timing for muscle protein synthesis around endurance work, and carbohydrate periodisation matched to training intensity. No calorie counting required. A framework that works with your schedule, not against it.

03

Recovery Protocol

The third element most hybrid athletes skip. A structured approach to sleep quality, HRV monitoring, deload weeks and active recovery sessions. Includes a weekly recovery checklist and guidance on interpreting your HRV trends to adjust training load before your body forces you to. Consistent training over 12 weeks beats perfect training for six.

04

Metrics Guide

What to track, when to track it, and what the numbers mean. VO2max estimates, HRV, weekly training load, resting heart rate, training distribution between zones. The data that tells you whether your training is working before your performance tells you. Covers both free and paid tracking options.
